## Products

### InvestSwipe

InvestSwipe is a mobile-first investing product built for young South Africans. It is designed to reduce complexity, improve financial access, and present investing in a format that feels native to a digital-first audience.

Key product themes:

- Swipe-based user experience
- Fractional investing access
- Short-form financial education
- Low-friction onboarding for first-time investors
- Beta target in Q3 2026 and public launch target in Q4 2026
